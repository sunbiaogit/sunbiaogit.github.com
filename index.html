In July, 2013 I joined the Department of Electrical Engineering & Automation, Tianjin University as an Lecturer.

Prior to this I received my PhD in the Department of Electronics Science & Technology at Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

My research interests lie in Machine Learning, Neural Information Processing, and Compressive Sensing.

PhD Advisor - Professor Jianjun Jiang was my advisor at Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

Current Research:

  • Precise plume capture in complex flow field based on spatially coupled compressed sensing. (2015-2017)
    Supported by National Natural Science Foundation (PI). This project intends to study the plume capture method based on spatially-coupled compressed sensing to get lower sampling rate and improve the accuracy.

  • Mind control of humanoid robots based on visual memory imagery mode. (2015-2018)
    Supported by National Natural Science Foundation (Participate). This study works at establishing a new electroencephalography (EEG) evoked mode named visual memory imagery mode to overcome the limitations of the existing evoked modes of EEG signals used in controlling humanoid robots.

Past Research:

  • Doctoral Research at Department of Electronic Science & Technology, Huazhong University of Science & Technology (research advisor: Dr. Jianjun Jiang).
    Research of signal spectrum measurement based on compressed sensing.
    Develop spectrum sensing algorithms for multi-tone and multi-band signals.
    Theoretical analysis of quantization principle and dynamic range of compressed sensing.
    Design an analogue-to-information converter at circuit level and system level based on random demodulation.

  • Master Research at Department of Electronic Science & Technology, Huazhong University of Science & Technology (research advisor: Dr. Jianjun Jiang).

    Research of quasi periodic array of active absorbing structure and microwave absorption characteristics of regulation mechanism.
    Develop microwave reflection measurement system using LabVIEW.
